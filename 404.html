About

Takenotes Design Studio is the design studio (desk in my living room) of me, Ryan Underwood. I am a non-award winning (It's all politics and I never submit anyways. I'm not bitter.) designer, leader, and somewhat of a front-end developer.

Over the course of my career I've been able to help companies, clients, and teams of all sizes build scalable design systems, branding, and web sites. I am the owner of Takenotes Design Studio which is a freelance studio I run to help companies build, define, or refine their design language.
