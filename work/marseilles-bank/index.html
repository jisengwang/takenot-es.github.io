Marseilles Bank

Updated branding for a local bank looking to become stronger.

The Problem

Marseilles Bank doesn't specialize in mortgages, real estate, auto loans, or personal loans. They specialize in support, trust, and loyalty. They found that they were losing customers to banks like Chase, Bank of America, and a slew of online banks.

The Solution

With the primary objective of building a design system that can compete with large commercial banks, I looked to what made Marseilles different.
